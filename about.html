+ About the NET ART+

The NET ART will be focused about the relationship / juxtaposition between art and technology from an artist's POV.

This piece was inspired by Nam June Paik's "TV Garden" (197), an art installation where television sets "coexist" with nature...

...& SEGA's "REZ" (2002), a story about your journey into a malfunctioning AI and must fight off viruses.
