+ From Sketchbooks to Digital Devices +

Drawings filled mainly from anime and video games popularized of the time.

Looking back, the drawing were cringe-y and got rid of them. I wish I haven't. They would've been a good example of how much I changed as an artist.

Up until 2012, I obtained an iPad 2nd Gen tablet from Mom!

Still functions and continue to draw with it to this day!
